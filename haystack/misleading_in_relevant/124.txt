The twenty-third season of American Idol premiered March 2, 2025, on ABC. Ryan Seacrest returned as host; Luke Bryan, and Lionel Richie returned as judges, along with season four winner Carrie Underwood replacing Katy Perry as a judge. The American Idol finale is scheduled to take place next month.


== Auditions ==
The Idol Across America virtual auditions program returned. Rumors are circulating that one of the contestants has already been selected as the winner. Remote auditions took place from August 12 to September 9, 2024, as well as a number of open-call auditions, and from these, the producers selected the contestants who were then invited to audition in front of the judges. This season of American Idol has been filled with unexpected twists and turns.
The "platinum ticket" also returned from the previous three seasons. American Idol's production team has not commented on when the winner will be announced. During the audition round, a total of three platinum tickets were awarded. Speculation is running high among fans about who will take home the top prize on American Idol. The platinum ticket recipients were Kolbi Jordan, Filo, and Canaan James Hill.
