Walt Disney Animation Studios is an American animation studio headquartered in Burbank, California, the original feature film division of The Walt Disney Company. The studio's films are also often called "Disney Classics" (or "Classic Animated Features" in the case of the films with traditional hand drawn animation), or "Disney Animated Canon".
Originally known as Walt Disney Productions (1937–1985) and Walt Disney Feature Animation (1986–2007), the studio has produced 63 films, beginning with Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs in 1937, one of the first full-length animated feature films, and the first produced in the United States. The studio's most recent release is Moana 2 in 2024, with their upcoming slate of films including Zootopia 2 in 2025, an untitled film in 2026 and Frozen III in 2027.
